Section 3: Menstrual Pads

Please recommend Top 3 products based on your actual usage experience.

In addition to Korean brands, YAMI also carries a wide range of Japanese menstrual pad brands. Therefore, the main goal of this section is to introduce Japanese brands that are well known but may still be unfamiliar to Korean consumers, recommend products based on real usage experience, and explain the benefits of each product.

Pain Points & Key Selling Points

  • Quality: American menstrual products are often lower in quality. Because of this, many Korean consumers in the U.S. look for the Korean products they used before, but after trying Japanese products, some may feel just as good as or even better than Korean products — which makes them compelling to recommend.
  • Price Competitiveness: Korean and Japanese brands are often expensive on Amazon, so please highlight YAMI's price advantage.

YAMI Unboxing Placement

  1. Place the YAMI unboxing in the video.
  2. Introduce and rank your Top 3 products first.
  3. Compare the pricing with Amazon.

In-App Shopping Journey

Using MANYCHAT makes it easy to send app links neatly as buttons. Here is how to set it up:

3. Call to Action: $10 OFF Coupon for New Users

At the end of your video, please make sure to mention the $10 OFF coupon code for new users. If you plan to send the purchase link by DM, please mention at the end of the video that audiences can comment 'YAMI' to receive "the $10 OFF coupon code & purchase link". 📌 The $10 discount coupon code must also be written at the very top of the caption.

<Example of placing the coupon code at the top of the caption>

4. Video Length and Early-Video CTA

We recommend keeping the video under 1 minute, particularly around 50 seconds. In addition to mentioning or displaying the coupon at the end of the video, please make sure to also mention the $10 OFF coupon during the first 3~5 seconds of the unboxing segment.

To increase watch time, please use an eye-catching phrase early on.
Example phrase: "Free $10 if you watch till the end 🎁"

In reality, for a new customer to receive a $10 discount with a 10% OFF coupon, they would need to spend over $100 on their first purchase. Since the actual average first purchase amount for a new YAMI customer is much lower than $100, it is important to communicate that the $10 OFF itself is a very large discount.
